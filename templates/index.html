About

Hi my name is Adriel. I've always liked creating things with computers and finding engaging projects to work on.

Most recently I'm building apps to help marketers engage with prospective customers and provide marketing departments insight into the success of their marking campaigns. In previous jobs I've built apps to help accountants build custom financial reports to monitor the health of hedge funds.

I like being on teams with a breadth of knowledge to solve a real world problem and build a product that will improve peoples' lives. Some tools I've worked with are Python, Java, Git, MongoDB, ReactJS, and CSS.

I enjoy working in Agile environments with a team focused on one singular goal: to build something great. At the end of the day I think its all about the people. I love hearing customer feedback and adapting products to best fit the user.
